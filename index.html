Xiangtao Kong (孔祥涛)

I am a Ph.D. student of PolyU supervised by Prof. Lei Zhang. I mainly focus on the Low-level Vision, Super-Resolution, and Interpretability of Deep Learning in the Image Processing field. I obtained my Honors B.Eng. degree from Shandong University in 2020 and my M.S. degree from X-Pixel at SIAT-CAS supervised by Prof. Chao Dong and Prof. Yu Qiao in 2023.
